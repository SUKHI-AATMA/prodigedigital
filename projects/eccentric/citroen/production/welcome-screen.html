WELCOME!

To get started connect to Citroen Remote App.

Follow these steps for setting up the connection:

  • Step 1:

    Install & open the Citroen Remote app on your Ipad. Make sure your Ipad and Display are connected to the same network.

  • Step 2:

    Tap on the Connect button on the upper right corner of your CITROEN REMOTE APP on the ipad.

  • Step 3:

    TAP ON ADD A NEW SCREEN AND POINT YOUR IPAD TO THIS SCREEN TO SCAN THE QR CODE.
