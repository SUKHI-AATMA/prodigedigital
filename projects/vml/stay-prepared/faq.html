Who is eligible to collect the free Vitamin D3 supplements?
  • We are distributing Vitamin D3 supplements to selected groups of Singaporeans as part of our wider campaign #StrongerwithSunshine, which aims to raise general awareness of the key role of Vitamin D.
  • Temasek Foundation paid for the production of the supplements, which were manufactured by BASF. The tablets are oral dispersing tablets which means they melt on the tongue and don’t need to be taken with water.
