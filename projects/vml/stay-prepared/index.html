About Vitamin D

  • What is Vitamin D?

    An essential micronutrient found in oily fish, eggs, and fortified food products

  • Why Take It?

    It is needed to regulate bodily functions such as calcium absorption.
    It also supports the immune system, and keeps bones and muscles strong.

  • How Do We Get It?

    There are two types of Vitamin D. We get Vitamin D2 from plants, and Vitamin D3 from animal food sources and when our skin is exposed to sunlight. Fortified foods are also sources of Vitamin D.
