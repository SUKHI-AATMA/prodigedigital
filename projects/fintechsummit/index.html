About us

Fintech Convergence Summit (FCS) intends to bring together the global FinTech ecosystem, to showcase their ideas and technologies that promote innovation in the financial services’ industry, help build relationships, create business and investment opportunities while bringing together industry professionals to discuss the effects of regulatory and policy shifts. With a prospect of over 1500+ attendees in this 3 days’ celebration, FCS is planned to be a mix of conference, workshops, investment zones, speed networking, roundtables, an exclusive CXOs’ dinner and awards

FCS aims to bring key stakeholders –Banking and financial institutions, policy makers, investors, technology giants, FinTechs, research institutions on a common platform to depth-charge the issues involved and the way forward. The multi-format festival will cover topics such as digital payments, lending, credit, insurance, digital banking, investments, blockchain, open banking, crypto assets, big data, personal finance, artificial intelligence, APIs etc.
