Our Mission

Ecological security is the foundation of sustainable and equitable development.
We are committed to strengthen, revive, and restore the process of ecological succession and the conservation of land, forest, and water resources in the country.

Read more

Our Core

Ecological Restoration

We pursue ecological security with rural communities at multiple levels, linking traditional and modern knowledge systems and methods that place these communities at the centre of every decision-making process.

Local Governance

We work towards a rights-based management system that fosters community ownership. We seek equitable environmental governance through collaboration, with the government and societies.

Enhancing Livelihoods

We believe that resilience can be found through systemic sustainability; through the better management of our commons and farming methods, and by building collective, effective local governance.
